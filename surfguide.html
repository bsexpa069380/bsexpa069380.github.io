Surf guide
Description

Surf guiding is a service provided by experienced surfers or guides who assist surfers in finding the best waves and locations based on their skill level and preferences.

Opening Hours

5.am - 6.pm

Location:

Arugam Bay local guide on the surf spot.

Duration

60 min

What's Included
  • Surf board
  • Rush guard
  • Both way ride /Tranportation
Additional Info

A surf guide offers a unique opportunity to enhance your surfing experience with expert knowledge and personalized support, ensuring a more enjoyable and rewarding time in the water.

Course Features
  • Local Knowledge: Access to insider knowledge of the best surf spots, including current wave conditions, tides, and local hazards.
  • Personalized Instruction: Tailored coaching based on your skill level and goals, whether you’re looking to improve technique, learn new maneuvers, or find the best waves.
  • Weather and Conditions: Up-to-date information on weather and surf conditions to ensure you have the best experience possible.
  • Equipment Advice: Guidance on the best equipment for your surfing needs, including board types and wetsuits suitable for local conditions.
Suitable For
  • Beginners: Those new to surfing who need help understanding local surf conditions and learning basic skills in a safe and supportive environment.
  • Intermediate Surfers: Surfers looking to refine their technique and find the best waves suited to their skill level.
  • Travelers: Visitors to a new surf destination who want to make the most of their time and get insider tips on the best spots and conditions.
How to Enroll
Contact Us: Reach out via phone, email, or by filling out the registration form on our website.
Choose Your Course Time: Select a convenient time based on the availability of the coach.
