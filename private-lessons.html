Private Lessons

Surf guide
Description

Private Surf Lessons offer one-on-one instruction aimed at providing personalized guidance and support for each student. These lessons are suitable for surfers of all levels, whether beginners or experienced riders. The goal of the course design is to tailor the instruction to each student's skill level and needs, ensuring that they can safely and effectively enhance their surfing techniques.

Opening Hours

5.am - 6.pm

Ages:

All Ages

Location:

Arugam Bay

Duration

90 min

What's Included
  • ISA certificated surf coach
  • Surf board
  • Rush guard
  • Both way ride /Tranportation
Additional Info

Course Features
  • One-on-One Instruction: Each student receives dedicated coaching, ensuring ample practice time and personalized guidance.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Course times and locations can be adjusted according to the student's preferences and needs.
  • Professional Coaches: All coaches are certified professionals with extensive teaching experience.
  • Equipment Support: High-quality surfboards and other necessary equipment are provided.
Suitable For
  • Beginners: Those looking to quickly learn the basics of surfing and safety.
  • Intermediate Surfers: Individuals aiming to improve their skills, refine their surfing posture, and enhance their techniques.
  • Advanced Surfers: Those seeking further challenges and advanced technique coaching.
How to Enroll
Contact Us: Reach out via phone, email, or by filling out the registration form on our website.
Choose Your Course Time: Select a convenient time based on the availability of the coach.
